Reservation System – Manages room bookings and availability.

Front Desk Management – Handles check-in, check-out, and guest services.

Housekeeping Module – Tracks room cleaning and maintenance.

Billing & Payments – Processes payments, invoices, and refunds.

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) – Stores guest details and preferences.
Point of Sale (POS) – Processes food orders, bills, and payments.

Table Management – Organizes seating and reservations.

Inventory Management – Tracks stock levels and supply orders.

Kitchen Display System (KDS) – Sends orders directly to the kitchen.

Online Ordering & Delivery – Manages digital food orders and deliveries.
